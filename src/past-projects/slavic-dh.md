Supported by Stanford's Division of Literatures, Cultures, and Languages, the [Slavic DH working group](https://digitalhumanities.stanford.edu/slavic/) brings together people using computational methods on Slavic-language materials, to test and compare tools and share tips and resources. I have turned its leadership over to graduate students in the department.
