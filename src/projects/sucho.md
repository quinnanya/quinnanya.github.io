I was one of the co-founders of [Saving Ukrainian Cultural Heritage Online (SUCHO)](https://www.sucho.org/), a rapid response web archiving project in response to Russia's full-scale invasion of Ukraine in February 2022.

SUCHO has won several major awards, including [Europa Nostra Cultural Heritage Champions](https://www.europeanheritageawards.eu/winners/saving-ukrainian-cultural-heritage-online-sucho/) in 2023 and the [Karl-Preusker-Medaille](https://www.bsb-muenchen.de/en/article/karl-preusker-medaille-2025-geht-an-den-ukrainischen-bibliotheksverband-besondere-wuerdigung-fuer-die-initiative-sucho-7076/) in 2025. It has served as a model for subsequent rapid response initiatives, including [Data Rescue](https://www.datarescueproject.org/) for US public data.
